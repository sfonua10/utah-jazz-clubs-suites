Luxury Suites

The best way to enjoy all the excitement!

Welcome to the very best Utah Sports and Entertainment has to offer - Vivint Smart Home Arena Luxury Suites. Whether you are entertaining business clients, closing a major deal or socializing with family and friends, a Luxury Suite provides all the convenience, comfort and service you desire. Home to the Utah Jazz and the most popular musical acts and family shows - Vivint Smart Home Arena is the place to meet, greet, see and be seen in Utah.

Why be a luxury suite holder?

Your suite can enhance your corporate profile and give you an advantage over your competitors. It offers the chance to communicate your message in a luxurious setting while making entertaining as easy as it is elegant. Be a part of the most unique and exciting way to entertain in Utah by becoming a Vivint Smart Home Arena Luxury Suite holder.

Executives Club

Enjoy the game in style.

The Vivint Smart Home Arena is the center stage for the top events in sports and entertainment in Utah! The most exclusive area to experience the excitement of the Utah Jazz, plus top performing acts and artists, is the Interform Executives Club on the suite level. This newly expanded area offers impeccable sightlines, in-seat service, and an exclusive VIP environment with an array of VIP benefits. The Interform Executives Club is the only place to entertain top clients, business associates, and friends in Utah's greatest sports and entertainment facility!

  • Four seats in the Interform Executives Club to every Utah Jazz preseason and regular season home game
  • Complimentary full-service in-suite VIP dinner, including soft drinks at all Utah Jazz games (alcoholic beverages available for purchase)
  • Private events with team coaches and management
  • Invitations to the annual Utah Jazz Hospitality Suite / Inerform Executives Club Golf Tournament
  • Private club (21 and older only)
  • Reserved club parking
  • Business dress code required
  • Plenty of room to network, socializes and "close that deal"
  • The first right to purchase your Interform Executive Club seats or other premium tickets for all other Vivint Smart Home Arena events
  • Luxury dining area with table service and seat service
  • Large flat screen TVs located throughout the club
  • Incredible Suite Level view for Jazz games

Lexus Club

Enjoy the game in style.
